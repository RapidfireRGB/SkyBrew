ingredientDictionary.py
    -Defines all Alchemy Ingredients and their
    respective Effects and Magnitudes as key value
    pairs in dictionaries. Also condenses these into
    a large dictionary at the bottom, which is what
    the main script searches through.

effectDictionary.py
    -Defines all Alchemy effects and their respective
    cost, magnitude, and duration as dictionaries.
    Also condenses these into a large dictionary at the
    bottom, which is what the main script searches
    through.
